# JourneyTrack - Daily Routine & Journey Tracker

A professional, privacy-focused web application that helps you track daily journeys and routines with beautiful visualizations and insights. All data is stored locally in your browser - nothing is ever sent to external servers.

## Features
### Journey Tracking
#### Comprehensive Logging
 - Record trips with transport mode, distance, time, and notes
#### Multiple Transport Modes
 - Walk, Cycle, Car, Bus, Train, and Other
#### CO₂ Savings Calculator
 - Track your environmental impact with automatic emissions calculations
#### Route Details
 - From/To locations with optional notes
### Dashboard & Analytics
#### Real-time Statistics
 - Today's journeys, distance, time, and CO₂ savings
#### Visual Charts
 - Weekly activity trends and transport distribution
#### Progress Tracking
 - Routine completion rates with visual indicators
#### Recent Activity
 - Quick overview of latest journeys
### Routine Management
#### Custom Routines
 - Create personalized daily routine items
#### Completion Tracking
 - Simple checkbox system with progress metrics
#### Daily Progress
 - Track completion rates and build consistent habits
#### Flexible Editing
 - Rename or remove routines as needed
### History & Insights
#### Advanced Filtering
 - Filter by date range and transport mode
#### Complete History
 - Review all past journeys with detailed information
#### Data Export
 - Download your complete history as JSON
#### Smart Pagination
 - Easy navigation through your journey history### Data Management
